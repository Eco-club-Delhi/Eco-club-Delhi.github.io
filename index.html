Welcome to the Eco Club

Our mission is simple: Youth for Earth. Through activism, education, and direct action, we aim to fight for a sustainable, green future.

Our Story

Our club draws inspiration from youth-led environmental movements, where the power of young people has driven meaningful change. We stand against systems that harm the environment, envisioning a world where nature is valued and protected.

Current Projects

  • Tree planting initiatives in local communities
  • Recycling drives and environmental awareness workshops
  • Advocacy campaigns against urban pollution

Significance of Our Symbols

Fist and Sapling: The fist represents youth empowerment and determination, while the sapling signifies the growth and restoration we aim to bring to the earth.

Leaf Flag: Minimalist design reflecting purity, nature, and a deep commitment to ecological consciousness. The leaf symbolizes the environmental focus of our mission.

Slogan: 'Reclaim the Earth' captures the essence of taking back our planet from destructive forces and restoring its natural balance.

Resources

We provide educational materials on environmental sustainability and resources for youth activists looking to make a difference in their communities.
