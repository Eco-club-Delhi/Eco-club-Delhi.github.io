Welcome to the Eco Club

Our mission is simple: Youth for Earth. Through activism, education, and direct action, we aim to fight for a sustainable, green future.

Our Story

Our club draws inspiration from eco-anarchist movements, where the power of youth has driven environmental change. We stand against systems of exploitation, envisioning a world where nature is valued and protected.

Current Projects

  • Tree planting initiatives in local communities
  • Recycling drives and environmental awareness workshops
  • Advocacy campaigns against urban pollution

Significance of Our Symbols

Fist and Sapling: The fist represents youth rebellion and power, while the sapling signifies the growth and restoration we aim to bring to the earth.

Leaf Flag: Minimalist design reflecting purity, nature, and anarchy. The leaf embodies the eco-consciousness at the heart of our mission.

Slogan: 'Reclaim the Earth' captures the essence of taking back our planet from destructive forces.
